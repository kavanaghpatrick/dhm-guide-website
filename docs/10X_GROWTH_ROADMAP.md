## Risks and Mitigation Strategies

### Risk 1: Algorithm Updates
**Probability:** Medium
**Impact:** High
**Mitigation:**
- Diversify content types (not just dosage guides)
- Build brand recognition and direct traffic
- Focus on E-E-A-T signals (expertise, authority, trust)
- Maintain high-quality, research-backed content

### Risk 2: Content Velocity Too Slow
**Probability:** Medium
**Impact:** High (delays timeline)
**Mitigation:**
- Use freelance writers to scale
- Create content templates for efficiency
- Batch research and writing
- Repurpose content across formats

### Risk 3: CTR Optimization Doesn't Deliver
**Probability:** Low
**Impact:** Medium
**Mitigation:**
- A/B test title variations
- Study high-CTR competitors
- Use SERP preview tools
- Iterate based on data

### Risk 4: New Content Doesn't Rank
**Probability:** Medium
**Impact:** Medium
**Mitigation:**
- Target low-competition keywords first
- Build internal linking structure
- Update and promote existing winners first
- Create supporting content clusters

### Risk 5: Budget Constraints
**Probability:** Medium
**Impact:** Medium (slows timeline)
**Mitigation:**
- Start with highest ROI activities (dosage guides)
- Use free tools where possible
- DIY content creation if needed
- Seek affiliate revenue to fund growth

### Risk 6: Market Saturation
**Probability:** Low
**Impact:** Low
**Mitigation:**
- DHM market is growing (13.5% CAGR)

- Opportunity for brand differentiation
- Expand into related niches (liver health, longevity)

### Risk 7: Competitor Response
**Probability:** Medium
**Impact:** Medium
**Mitigation:**
- Build moat with comprehensive content
- Focus on unique value (calculators, tools)
