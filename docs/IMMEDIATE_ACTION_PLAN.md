## Week 2: Create First Dosage Guide (12 hours)

### Target: "DHM Dosage by Body Weight Calculator" Guide

**Why this topic:**
- GSC shows queries: "how much dhm to take", "dhm dosage", "dihydromyricetin dosage"
- Current top guide doesn't break down by body weight
- Adds unique value (calculator)
- Low competition keyword

**Content Structure (2,500 words):**

1. **Introduction** (300 words)
   - Why body weight matters for DHM dosage
   - Quick answer: 3-5mg per kg body weight

2. **Body Weight Dosage Chart** (400 words)
   - 100-130 lbs: 200-300mg
   - 130-160 lbs: 300-400mg
   - 160-190 lbs: 400-500mg
   - 190-220 lbs: 500-600mg
   - 220+ lbs: 600-800mg
   - Include metric conversions

   - Drink type (beer vs liquor)
   - Frequency of drinking
   - Gender differences
   - Asian flush considerations

5. **Dosage Examples by Scenario** (600 words)
   - Light social drinking (2-3 drinks)
   - Moderate drinking (4-6 drinks)
   - Heavy drinking (7+ drinks)
   - Wine vs beer vs liquor dosing

6. **Safety and Side Effects** (300 words)
   - Maximum safe dosage
   - When to avoid high doses
   - Signs of too much DHM

7. **FAQ Section** (200 words)
   - 5-8 common questions with concise answers

## Content Creation Templates

### Title Tag Formula

**Dosage Guides:**
- "[Topic] Dosage Guide [Year]: How Much to Take [Benefit]"
- "How Much [Supplement] to Take: Complete Dosage Guide [Year]"
- "[Supplement] Dosage by [Variable]: [Benefit] Guide"

Examples:
- "DHM Dosage by Body Weight: Complete Guide 2025"
- "How Much DHM to Take: Dosage Guide for Hangovers"
- "DHM Dosage for Heavy Drinking: Maximum Protection Guide"

**Comparisons:**
- "[Product A] vs [Product B]: Which is Better for [Benefit]? [Year]"
- "[Supplement A] vs [Supplement B]: Complete Comparison [Year]"
- "[Brand A] vs [Brand B]: Honest Review & Comparison"

Examples:
- "DHM vs Milk Thistle: Which is Better for Liver? 2025"
- "No Days Wasted vs Cheers: Complete Comparison 2025"
- "Flyby vs Good Morning Pills: Honest Review & Comparison"

**Reviews:**
- "[Product] Review [Year]: Does It Work? [Tested/Ranked/Evidence]"
- "[Brand] [Product] Review: Honest Analysis [Year]"
- "Best [Category] [Year]: [X] Products Tested & Ranked"

Examples:
- "Flyby Recovery Review 2025: Does It Work? [Tested]"
- "DHM1000 Review: Honest Analysis of Double Wood's Formula"
- "Best DHM Supplements 2025: 15 Brands Tested & Ranked"

### Meta Description Formula

**Structure:** [Quick Answer] + [Benefit/Proof] + [CTA]

**Dosage Guides:**
"[Recommended dosage range]. Learn optimal [supplement] dosing for [benefit] based on [factor]. Includes calculator and research-backed protocols."

Example:
"Take 3-5mg DHM per kg body weight. Learn optimal dosing for hangover prevention based on weight, alcohol type, and drinking frequency. Includes calculator."

**Comparisons:**
"[Product A] vs [Product B]: We compared [aspects]. See which is better for [benefit], [factor], and [factor]. Honest analysis backed by research."

Example:
"DHM vs Milk Thistle: We compared effectiveness, dosage, and research. See which is better for liver protection and hangover prevention. Honest analysis."

**Reviews:**
"[Tested/Analyzed] [Product]. See our verdict on effectiveness, ingredients, [aspect], and [aspect]. Includes comparison to [X] alternatives."

Example:
"We tested Flyby Recovery with real hangovers. See our verdict on effectiveness, ingredients, dosage, and value. Includes comparison to 5 alternatives."

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

1. **Not updating prerendered pages** - Remember Pattern #11 from CLAUDE.md
2. **Keyword stuffing in title tags** - Keep them natural and compelling
3. **Creating content without search intent validation** - Check GSC data first
4. **Ignoring mobile optimization** - Most traffic is mobile
5. **Not submitting to GSC after changes** - Speed up indexing
6. **Copying competitor content** - Google penalizes duplicate content
7. **Over-optimizing** - Focus on user value, not just SEO
8. **Not tracking changes** - You need data to know what works
