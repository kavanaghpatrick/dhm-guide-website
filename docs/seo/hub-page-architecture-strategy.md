# Hub Page Architecture Strategy for DHM Guide Website

**Created:** 2025-10-21
**Purpose:** Organize 169 blog posts into a strategic hub page hierarchy to improve SEO, distribute link equity, and enhance user experience

---

## Executive Summary

This document outlines a comprehensive 3-tier hub page architecture designed to:
- **Organize 169 blog posts** into logical topic clusters
- **Distribute link equity** from high-authority pages to individual posts
- **Improve internal linking** and crawlability
- **Enhance user experience** with clear content pathways
- **Boost SEO performance** through topic authority and relevance signals

**Current State:**
- 169 active blog posts in `/never-hungover/`
- 7 main navigation pages (Home, Guide, Reviews, Compare, Research, Never Hungover, About)
- Limited internal linking between blog posts
- No category/topic organization beyond tags

**Proposed Architecture:**
- **Tier 1:** 4 top-level hub pages (cornerstone content)
- **Tier 2:** 8 category hub pages (topic clusters)
- **Tier 3:** 169 individual blog posts (leaf content)
