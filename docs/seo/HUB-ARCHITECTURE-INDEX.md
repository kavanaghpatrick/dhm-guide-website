## ğŸ“ Key Concepts

### What is a Hub Page?
A hub page is a comprehensive resource that organizes and links to multiple related pieces of content. It serves as both:
- **Navigation aid** for users to discover related content
- **SEO signal** to search engines about topic expertise
- **Link equity distributor** passing authority to child pages

### Why 3 Tiers?
- **Tier 1:** Broad, high-authority cornerstone content
- **Tier 2:** Specialized topic clusters for specific audiences
- **Tier 3:** Detailed, long-tail content answering specific questions

This structure mirrors Google's understanding of topical hierarchy and user search intent.

### Internal Linking Strategy
Every page should link:
- **UP** to parent hubs (breadcrumbs)
- **ACROSS** to related content (contextual links)
- **DOWN** to conversion pages (CTAs)

This creates a web of connections that benefits both users and SEO.
