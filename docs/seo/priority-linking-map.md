## Link Placement Best Practices

### 1. Hub Link Placement (Breadcrumb)
**Location:** Top of post, after H1 title
**Format:**
```
This post is part of our [Complete DHM Safety Guide Hub](link) - your comprehensive resource for DHM safety, research, and guidelines.
```

### 2. Contextual Links (In-Content)
**Location:** Within relevant paragraphs
**Best Practices:**
- Anchor text matches destination page title
- Links appear natural, not forced
- Maximum 1 link per 150-200 words
- Links appear in first 50% of content (higher engagement)

### 3. Related Posts Section (End of Post)
**Location:** Before comments, after conclusion
**Format:**
