## PART 2: KEY INSIGHTS & PATTERNS

### Insight #1: The Science Translation Gap (Gemini)

**The Problem:**
Your site attracts users at all funnel stages but only serves one stage well:
- **Awareness (FAILING):** "is dhm safe" queries ‚Üí academic RCT page = 0% CTR
- **Consideration (WINNING):** "dhm vs milk thistle" ‚Üí comparison page = 8.74% CTR
- **Decision (FAILING):** "best dhm supplement" ‚Üí generic /reviews page = 0% CTR

**The Root Cause:**
You've compiled scientific data but failed to translate it for consumers. The science *should* build authority and trust, but instead it's isolated and non-performing.

**The Solution:**
- **Create Consumer-Facing Hub:** "How DHM Works: Science Explained Simply"
- **Translate Key Findings:** Summaries, infographics, clear takeaways (not academic language)
- **Use as Credibility Source:** Link FROM consumer page TO dense scientific pages
- **Frame It Right:** "Our conclusions are based on the following research [link]"

**Impact:**
Transforms scientific content from liability into authority-building asset. Satisfies consumer query AND provides depth for those who want it.

---

### Insight #2: E-E-A-T Fragmentation (Gemini)

**The Problem:**
The site has pockets of authority (dosage, comparisons) but they're not connected. This prevents Google from recognizing topical authority across the site.

**The Evidence:**
- Dosage pages perform exceptionally (14.67% CTR) but don't link to scientific backing
- Comparison pages rank well but don't leverage scientific authority
- Scientific pages exist but aren't referenced by high-performing pages

**The Solution:**
- **Build Topic Clusters:** Dosage Hub ‚Üí Comparison Hub ‚Üí Science Hub ‚Üí Best DHM Hub
- **Strategic Internal Linking:** High-authority pages (dosage) link TO money pages (Best DHM)
- **Demonstrate Breadth:** Show Google you cover the topic comprehensively

**Impact:**
Cohesive topical authority structure can boost ALL page rankings, not just individual pages.

---

### Insight #3: Content as Liability vs Asset (Gemini)

**The Revelation:**
Zero-click, high-impression pages aren't neutral - they're HARMING the site. They send negative signals to Google that your site doesn't satisfy queries, which can suppress rankings site-wide.

**The Math:**
- 2,480 impressions generating 1 click = 0.04% CTR
- Expected CTR at position 6-9: 3-7% (75-175 clicks)
- Shortfall: 74-174 clicks lost = negative quality signal

**The Risk:**
Google's Helpful Content System devalues sites with high impressions but low engagement. These pages could be dragging down your entire domain authority.

**The Fix:**
- **30-Day Test:** Rewrite titles/metas for consumer audience
- **Delete if No Improvement:** If CTR doesn't reach 2%+ in 30 days, 301 redirect to commercial pages
- **Repurpose Strategically:** Extract science snippets into callout boxes on money pages
