## SUCCESS PATTERNS TO REPLICATE

### What Works (CTR >5%)
1. **Direct answer in title** - "How Much DHM to Take" (34.62% CTR)
2. **Brand comparisons** - "No Days Wasted vs Cheers" (11.11% CTR)
3. **"Best" keywords** - Target 8%+ CTR
4. **Position 4-7** - Sweet spot for CTR optimization
5. **Specific over general** - "for hangover" outperforms generic queries

### What Fails (CTR 0%)
1. **Generic reviews** without comparison angle
2. **Scientific query targeting** without direct answers upfront
3. **Positions 8+** - Need to improve or delete
4. **Off-topic content** (Italian drinking culture, etc.)
5. **Duplicate content** competing with own pages

## IMPLEMENTATION NOTES

### Content Creation Principles:
1. **Direct answer FIRST** - No fluff before the answer
2. **Comparison tables** - Visual, scannable, data-rich
3. **FAQ schema** - All new pages get structured data
4. **Internal linking** - Link to comparison hub and related guides
5. **Calculator/interactive elements** where applicable
6. **"Best for..." callouts** - Help users self-select

### SEO Technical:
1. **Title format:** "[Primary Keyword]: [Benefit/Answer] ([Year/Qualifier])"
2. **Meta description:** Direct answer + CTA in <155 chars
3. **H1 = Title** (exact match or close variation)
4. **URL structure:** /never-hungover/[primary-keyword-phrase]
5. **Image optimization:** Alt text with target keywords
6. **Internal links:** 3-5 contextual links per page
