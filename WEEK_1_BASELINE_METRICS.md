## RISK MITIGATION

### Potential Negative Signals:
1. **"Pogo-sticking"**: Users click but immediately bounce
   - **Monitor:** Bounce rate in Google Analytics
   - **Fix:** Ensure title matches content promise

2. **Position Drops**: New titles confuse ranking signals
   - **Monitor:** Weekly position tracking
   - **Fix:** Revert to baseline if drop >3 spots

3. **Decreased Impressions**: Title changes hurt rankings
   - **Monitor:** Impression trends
   - **Fix:** Investigate for algorithm issues

### Rollback Plan:
If any page shows severe negative impact (CTR drop >30% OR position drop >5 spots):
1. Revert that specific page's title/meta to original
2. Document why it failed
3. Test alternative title variation
4. Keep successful changes on other pages
