SIMPLIFIED PLAN (APPROVED)
================================================================================

PHASE 1: Data Collection (30-45 minutes)
- Export Search Console data for all 9 posts (impressions, clicks, position)
- Tier posts: HIGH (10+ impressions), MEDIUM (5-10), LOW (<2)
- Analyze top 3 SERP competitors for word count, structure, citations
- Pull "People Also Ask" data for each keyword
- Set data-driven word count targets (not 1,200-1,500 generic)

PHASE 2: Pilot Expansion (1.5-2 hours for 2-3 posts)
- Expand only top 2-3 posts by traffic potential
- Use competitive word counts (could be 800-2,500, not arbitrary 1,200)
- Add FAQ sections only if PAA data supports them
- Add 3-5 peer-reviewed citations
- Add 2-3 internal links to related content
- Per-post effort: 45-60 minutes (not 20-30)

PHASE 3: Measurement (4-6 weeks)
- Monitor Search Console for pilot posts
- Target: 25%+ click increase or 50%+ impression increase
- Calculate ROI
- If successful: Scale Phase 2 to remaining 6 posts
- If unsuccessful: Evaluate delete/redirect strategy

PHASE 4 (Optional): Delete/Redirect
- Tier 3 posts (0-2 impressions) may be better served by 301-redirects
- Identify stronger content covering the same topic
- Redirect thin posts to authoritative pages
